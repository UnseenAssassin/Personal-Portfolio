My Projects

StudentSwap

StudentSwap is a marketplace platform exclusively for university students to buy, sell, or trade items like electronics, furniture, and textbooks. It requires a valid student email to sign-up, ensuring a trusted, campus only community. Its purpose is to make it easier and safer for students to exchange goods locally. It solves the problem of unreliable, general public platforms by creating a student-focused, verified enviorment tailored to campus life.

Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Powershell, Firebase

20 Questions

A web-based twist on the classic guessing game. The app uses a decision tree algorithm to try and guess the object you're thinking of in 20 questions or less. Users can train the AI by submitting new questions and answers.

Technologies: JavaScript, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, EJS

Live Demo GitHub

Lofi Focus Timer

A minimalistic productivity app based on the Pomodoro Technique. It features adjustable focus/break intervals and an embedded lofi radio player to help users stay relaxed and productive while working.
